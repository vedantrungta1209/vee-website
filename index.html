Our beginning

Food is more than fuel.
It's feeling, memory,
and connection.

We built Vee because we believed delivery could be more than a transaction. Every meal has a kitchen behind it, a chef with intent, and a diner with expectations. We exist to honor all three — with care baked into every layer.

We partner only with restaurants that cook with integrity — places where the owner knows their suppliers by name. We believe the people who bring food to your door deserve dignity, fair compensation, and respect. These aren't policies. They're principles we won't negotiate on.
